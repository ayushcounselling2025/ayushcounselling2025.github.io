The counselling process has 3 major rounds:

Offline counselling to fill vacant seats after round 2.

Here are some frequently asked questions:

Yes, NEET is mandatory for all AYUSH courses.

Yes, both options are available during choice filling.

You may lose the seat and your security deposit.
